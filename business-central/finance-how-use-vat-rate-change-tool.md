### <a name="to-prepare-vat-rate-change-conversions"></a><span data-ttu-id="90af7-120">Pour préparer les conversions de modification du taux de TVA</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="90af7-120">To prepare VAT rate change conversions</span></span>
